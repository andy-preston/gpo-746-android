GPO-746 Telephone Control Electronics With USB Interface

Dial connection

We use blue/grey to detect dial mode and pink/orange to count pulses. It may look simpler to use orange/brown and detect both switches with one data line.

But we use the mode change as an opportunity to zero the counter and to signal that the count is complete.

USB 5V Power

Shown in the schematic as a switch, we have a jumper to connect the USB 5V power line to the power rail of the microcontrollers.

This should be left open when the 20V supply and 7805 are connected.

It can be closed when the microcontroller USB link are being tested independently of the switching and amplifier circuits.
